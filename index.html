About



Card Whisper is a mobile application created for blind and visually impaired users. Designed with accessibility in mind, Card Whisper uses talk to text technology to audibly read playing cards to the user.

The application utilizes a Google API and your device’s camera to identify the card within seconds. Card Whisper was developed by students of Academy Pittsburgh.

How It Works


Card Whisper is able to identify and tell the user any suit of clubs, diamonds, hearts and spaces in a deck of standard playing cards.

Card Whisper will be able to read the card and return the identification with the suit and number or face of the playing card.

It is recommended to be used with headphones during competitive card play, as Card Whisper will read the cards aloud to the user as it recognizes them. 

Instructions
Open Card Whisper on your IOS or Android Device. 


Hold your playing card over the phone. The app automatically turns on your device's camera. 


Press anywhere on the device's screen to take a picture of the card. A sound will notify you that the picture has been taken. 


After a few seconds, Card Whisper will identify your card for you. 


The app will continue to look for cards to read, so you can hold the next card over your phone whenever you're ready.
